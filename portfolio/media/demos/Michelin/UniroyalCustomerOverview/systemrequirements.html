System Requirements  
 

This browser does not meet the system requirements needed to run this course. Please be sure you are running one of the supported browsers listed below and that Adobe Flash version 10.1 or higher is installed.

Flash Version Required: 10.1

Web Browsers Supported:

Google Chrome
Firefox 4 and higher
Internet Explorer 8 and higher
Safari 5 or higher

Devices Supported:

Desktop PC & Mac running the above browsers.
iPad and Android tablets.
Smart phones are not supported at this time.
